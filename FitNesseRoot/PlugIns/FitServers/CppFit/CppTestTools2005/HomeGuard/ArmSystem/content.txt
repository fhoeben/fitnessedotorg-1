When Home Guard is not armed, pressing the arm button arms the system.  Pressing it again disarms the system.

|!-HomeGuardFixtures.CheckFrontPanel-!|
|armed|visual alarm|siren|display|
|off|off|off|READY|

|!-HomeGuardFixtures.Control-!|
|press arm button|

|!-HomeGuardFixtures.CheckFrontPanel-!|
|armed|visual alarm|siren|display|
|on|off|off|ARMED|

|!-HomeGuardFixtures.Control-!|
|press arm button|

|!-HomeGuardFixtures.CheckFrontPanel-!|
|armed|visual alarm|siren|display|
|off|off|off|READY|
